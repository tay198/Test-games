How to Play Taylan's Game!

Help Stacey collect... items... by clicking her image before time runs out! The items will move, so be quick! Each click on Stacey earns points and builds your combo for bigger scores. Avoid Officer Jake at all costs - clicking him means Game Over! Jake moves too! Watch out for Innocent Bystanders too; clicking them will cost you points. Grab Power-Ups for special boosts!
